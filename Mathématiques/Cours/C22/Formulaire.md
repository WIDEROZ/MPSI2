e_{i}^{*}(x_{j})
\end{pmatrix}_{i, j}$$
$$Mat_{e, f}(u) = Mat_{f}(u(e))$$
Pour : 
$$\begin{cases}
A = Mat_{e, f}(u) \\
X = Mat_{e}(x) \\
Y = Mat_{f}(u(x))
\end{cases}$$
