$$v = (1-x_{v})v_{l} + x_{v}v_{v}$$

$$v = v_{l} + x_{v}(v_{v}-v_{l})$$
Alors, 
$$x_{v} = \frac{v - v_{l}}{v_{v} - v_{l}}$$
