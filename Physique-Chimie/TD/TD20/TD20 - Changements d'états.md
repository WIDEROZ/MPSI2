$$x_{l} = \frac{m_{l}}{m_{l} + m_{v}}$$
$$V = V_{l} + V_{g} \Leftrightarrow (m_{l} + m_{v})v = m_{l}v_{l} + m_{v}v_{v} \Leftrightarrow v = x_{l}v_{l} + x_{v}v_{v}$$
Alors, 
$$v = (1-x_{v})v_{l} + x_{v}v_{v}$$
Alors, 
$$v = v_{l} + x_{v}(v_{v}-v_{l})$$
Alors, 
$$x_{v} = \frac{v - v_{l}}{v_{v} - v_{l}}$$
